I am a scientific collaborator in the Labour and Health Section of the State Secretariat for Economic Affairs (SECO) and an Associate Researcher at the Institute for Sociological Research (IRS) of the University of Geneva. I am a member of the editorial team of the journal ***Les Mondes du Travail*** and hold a Maître de conférences (MCF) qualification for the section 19 (sociology, demography) issued by the Conseil national des universités (CNU) in France.

This blog gathers some open data short analysis on the Swiss Labour Force, wile all my scientific works and activities are available on my website [here](https://www.nicolacianferoni.ch/).
